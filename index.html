Purpose

The purpose of our app is to provide a cheaper and shorter eye test for people who possibly have autism.

Current autism eye tests:

Take between 30 and 60 minutes

Can cost up to $1000-$2000

Take 1.5-4 hours for results

Did You Know?

One-fourth of children with autism are undiagnosed.

Our Project

Is free to use and takes only a few minutes to test and get your results back.

How To Use

1) On one of our web pages, click the app link and you will get redirected to a new page.

2) Once the red dot starts moving, simply click on the boxes around the screen until the color is green. This will calibrate the camera and track your eye movements.

3) After all of the buttons turn green, click on the middle START button.

4) Read the instructions on the next page and click START once your ready.

5) After 5 seconds, your test will be complete and your eye tracking data will be put into our AI machine learning model.

6) Wait a few seconds and you should be able to see your test results as well as a few recommendations for the future!

Autism

  • Autism (Autism Spectrum Disorder) is a developmental disability in the brain. In 2021, approximately 1 in 44 children in the United States were diagnosed with autism. Autism drastically effects the way people learn, move, pay attention, and overall behave. As a result, its import to be able to find out if you or a loved one possibly has autism to maxamize your learning potential and life skills.

    • Autism Testing

  • Currently autism is tested by pediatricians by observing and asking questions to the parent. However, these tests can occasioanlly cost between $1000-$2000, can take upwards of 1 to 2 hours, and take an additional 1.5-4 hours for results.

  • In recent research by Washington State University, they found that eye patterns as well as pupillary reactions to light can potentially be used as a faster a quicker method of autism testing. Our project expands on this idea and compares eye patterns other autism patients, to see if you possibly have autism.
