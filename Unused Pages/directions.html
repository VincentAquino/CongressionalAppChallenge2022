App Info

Purpose

The purpose of our app is to provide a cheaper and shorter eye test for people who possibly have autism.


Current autism eye tests:

- Take between 30 and 60 minutes

- Can cost up to $1000 - $2000

- Take 1.5-4 hours for results


Our project is free to use, and takes only a few minutes to complete the test and get your results back.


How to Use

1) On one of our web pages, click the app link and you will get redirected to a new page.

2) Once the red dot starts moving, simply click on the boxes around the screen until the color is green. This will calibrate the camera and track your eye movements.

3) After all of the buttons turn green, click on the middle START button.

4) Read the instructions on the next page and click START once your ready.

5) After 5 seconds, your test will be complete and your eye tracking data will be put into our AI machine learning model.

6) Wait a few seconds and you should be able to see your test results as well as a few recommendations for the future!

7) Feel free to do this eye tracking test a few times!
